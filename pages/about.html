About us

We started this organization to help hospitals increase
resources for patients such as supplies and books for
children as well as to support research funding by
organizing fun, interactive tennis tournaments for
players. We found two things we shared a passion for,
helping the medical community and tennis, and
formed Tennis For Texas.
