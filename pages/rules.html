Rules

Rules

- Pro-set to 8 games with no-ad
- Normal tiebreak to 7 points
- If the tiebreak score is 6-all, keep playing until one player is winning by 2 points

Format

- Single Elimination Bracket
- Players that lose in the first round move to the consolation bracket.
- Any players that lose after the 1st round are eliminated.
- All competitors are required to show up to the tournament - site 10 minutes before their scheduled match
